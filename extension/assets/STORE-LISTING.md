## Full Description

### Optimize Your Amazon Titles with AI

TitlePerfect analyzes your Amazon product titles and generates optimized variations that score higher for SEO, Amazon Rufus AI compatibility, and buyer conversions.

**How it works:**
1. Navigate to any Amazon product page
2. Open TitlePerfect from the side panel
3. Get instant compliance checks and score breakdowns
4. Receive AI-generated title variations ranked by performance

**Key Features:**
- SEO, Rufus AI, and Conversion scoring with detailed breakdowns
- Amazon title compliance checking (character limits, banned words, category rules)
- AI-generated title variations with reasoning explanations
- Mobile search preview showing how your title appears on phones
- Competitor ASIN analysis (Pro)
- One-click copy for any title variation
- Works on all Amazon marketplaces (US, UK, CA, DE, FR, IT, ES, IN, JP)

**Free Plan:** 10 title analyses per month
**Pro Plan:** Unlimited analyses, competitor analysis, priority support

### Privacy
TitlePerfect only reads product data from Amazon pages you visit. We do not collect browsing history or personal data beyond what is needed for the service. See our full privacy policy at https://titleperfect.app/privacy.

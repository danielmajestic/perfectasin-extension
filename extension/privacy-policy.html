Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 14, 2026

TitlePerfect ("we", "us", "our") operates the TitlePerfect Chrome browser extension and related services at titleperfect.app. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

When you create an account we collect your email address and authentication credentials through Firebase Authentication. If you sign in with Google, we receive your name and email from your Google account.

1.2 Amazon Product Data

When you use TitlePerfect on an Amazon product page, the extension reads publicly visible product information from that page, including:

  • Product title, ASIN, brand, category, price
  • Bullet points and product images
  • Rating and review count

This data is sent to our API server for analysis. We do not access your Amazon account, purchase history, or any information not visible on the public product page.

1.3 Usage Data

We collect anonymous usage analytics (via Google Analytics 4) including:

  • Feature usage events (analyses run, variations copied)
  • Error events for debugging
  • Session duration and extension interactions

Analytics data does not include product titles, ASINs, or any Amazon product information.

1.4 Payment Information

Payment processing is handled entirely by Stripe. We do not store credit card numbers or bank details. We receive only your subscription status and plan type from Stripe.

3. Data Storage and Retention

  • Account data: Retained while your account is active. Deleted within 30 days of account deletion request.
  • Analysis results: Retained for up to 90 days to provide analysis history. Free-tier analyses are not stored beyond the session.
  • Analytics data: Retained for 14 months per Google Analytics defaults.
  • Local storage: The extension stores preferences and cached data locally in your browser via chrome.storage.local. This data never leaves your device unless you initiate an analysis.

5. Data Security

We protect your data with:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data in transit
  • Firebase Authentication with secure token management
  • Server-side API authentication via Firebase ID tokens
  • No storage of raw credentials in the extension

6. Your Rights

6.1 All Users

  • Access: Request a copy of your data at any time
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Correction: Update your account information
  • Portability: Export your analysis history

6.2 GDPR (European Economic Area)

If you are in the EEA, you have additional rights under the General Data Protection Regulation:

  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to object to processing based on legitimate interest
  • Right to withdraw consent at any time
  • Right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority

6.3 CCPA (California)

If you are a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act provides you with:

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to opt out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell personal information)
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising your rights

7. Cookies and Tracking

The extension itself does not use cookies. Google Analytics uses a locally stored client ID (not a cookie) to associate events within a session. You can disable analytics by uninstalling the extension.

8. Children's Privacy

TitlePerfect is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If we learn that we have collected data from a child under 13, we will delete it promptly.
